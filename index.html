About

As an internal IT Support Analyst for a ecommerce company, I collaborate with all team members and directors in understanding their departmental needs with technology and software, maintain internal systems, and build and maintain vendor relationship.

I graduated with a degree in Housing and Development providing me with a unique design perspective towards technology. I have always had a knack for computers, but never explore the career field until taking a coding class in Java. This solidified my decision in going back to school for a Masters in Computer Science, and I’ve now worked in IT for two years. In continuing with my career growth, I am looking to further myself as a full stack developer and become immersed within the web development industry and set my path towards creating an emerging market combining both technology and architecture.
